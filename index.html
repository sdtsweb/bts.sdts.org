About

About Bharathiyar Tamil School

Our Mission

At Bharathiyar Tamil School, our goal is to introduce children from the age of three years and up to Tamil language and culture. Classes are taught in a fun, engaging, stress-free environment, with an emphasis on conversation skills, reading, and writing.

  • Completely volunteer-run program, dependent on diligent volunteering by parents & other interested volunteers.
  • Classes are held on Fridays between 6:30 p.m. & 8:30 p.m. at Shoal Creek Elementary School in Carmel Mountain Ranch.
  • BTS calendar year follows the Poway School District's regular school calendar.

Our Approach

The school follows the comprehensive, structured syllabus developed by the American Tamil Academy. We create opportunities for children to:

  • Participate in cultural activities such as plays and performances
  • Display their budding skills on stage
  • Interact with community thought leaders
  • Learn Tamil in an immersive environment

School Year

Academic Calendar and Programs

Our academic year includes structured classes, interactive sessions, and assessments to ensure students progress in their understanding and usage of Tamil. Key dates and schedules are updated regularly to keep our community informed.

Faculty

Our Dedicated Teachers

Our Teaching Team

Our faculty consists of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about teaching Tamil language and culture. Each class is led by experienced teachers who create an engaging and supportive learning environment.

  • Multiple teachers per class for personalized attention
  • Regular communication with parents about student progress
  • Focus on interactive learning and cultural immersion
  • Experienced in teaching various age groups and proficiency levels

Teaching Approach

Our teachers employ various teaching methods to make learning engaging and effective:

  • Classroom Activities:
    • Weekly vocabulary and conversation practice
    • Interactive storytelling and songs
    • Cultural activities and performances
    • Regular assessments and feedback
